1. ENGLISH

  1.1 Info

      Welcome to Hurrican

      Hurrican is a freeware jump and shoot game created by Poke53280 that is based on the Turrican game series.
      Blast your way through nine action-packed levels filled with different enemies and powerups.

      You can learn the basic controls of the game by playing the tutorial level.
      Just select "start game" and choose "play tutorial" in the main menu.

  1.3 System requirements

      Windows XP or higher
      512 MB RAM
      DirectX 8.1 or higher
      GeForce3 (or equivalent) or higher graphics card with at least 128MB RAM

  1.4 Troubleshooting

      If you have any problems playing hurrican, feel free to contact us at
      information@poke53280.de
      or visit http://www.hurrican-game.de for technical support.
